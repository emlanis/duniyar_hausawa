# Privacy Policy for Duniyar Hausawa

**Last Updated: November 2025**

---

## Overview

Your privacy is important to us. Duniyar Hausawa ("the App") is designed with privacy as a priority. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

**Key Point:** This app stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

---

## 1. Data Collection

Duniyar Hausawa **does not collect** or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data is stored locally on your device only.

We do not:
- Collect personal information
- Track your usage
- Send data to remote servers
- Use analytics services
- Display advertisements

---

## 2. Information We Store Locally

The following information is stored **only on your device** using standard mobile storage:

- **Quiz Results**: Your quiz scores and completion history
- **Favorites**: Proverbs and images you've marked as favorites
- **Progress**: Learning streaks and achievements
- **Preferences**: App settings and customization options

**Storage Methods:**
- SQLite database (for structured data)
- SharedPreferences (for app settings)

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We **do not share, sell, or transmit** your data to any third parties. Everything stays on your device.

There are no:
- Third-party analytics
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud backups (unless you use device-level backups)

---

## 4. Data Security

Your data is secure because it never leaves your device. We use standard mobile operating system security features to protect your locally stored data.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

- **View**: All data is accessible within the app
- **Delete**: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
- **Export**: Data is not exported or backed up externally
- **Modify**: You can change or remove favorites and clear quiz history

---

## 6. Internet Connection

This app works **completely offline**. No internet connection is required or used. All content (proverbs, images, quizzes) is bundled with the app.

---

## 7. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party services, including:
- Analytics platforms
- Advertising networks
- Social media APIs
- Cloud storage services
- Payment processors (app is free)

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

The app complies with:
- COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- Other applicable privacy laws

---

## 9. Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|------------|---------|----------|
| Storage | Save quiz results and favorites locally | Yes |
| Notifications | Send daily proverb reminders (opt-in) | No |

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and never for data collection.

---

## 10. Device Backups

If you enable device-level backups (iCloud, Google Drive), your locally stored app data may be included in those backups. This is controlled by your device settings, not by our app.

---

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in app updates. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new privacy policy.
